<p><p>The paper-based diagnostic test kits market encompasses several types, including lateral flow assay test kits, dipstick test kits, and microfluidic paper-based systems. Lateral flow assays utilize capillary action to deliver results quickly, often seen in home pregnancy tests. Dipstick kits are simple, portable tests where a strip is immersed in a sample, producing colorimetric changes for analysis. Microfluidic paper-based systems integrate advanced fluid management, enabling multiple tests on a single strip, enhancing sensitivity and versatility in diagnostics.</p></p>
