<p><ul><li>Whole Milk</li><li>Skim Milk</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial milk powder market is primarily divided into two main types: whole milk powder and skim milk powder. Whole milk powder contains the full fat content of milk, making it suitable for applications that require creaminess and richness, such as confectionery and bakery products. In contrast, skim milk powder is created by removing the fat, resulting in a lower-calorie option ideal for products like dietary supplements and non-fat dairy applications. Each type serves distinct uses in the food industry.</p></p>
