<p><p>A Heatless Regenerative Desiccant Dryer is a specialized equipment designed to remove moisture from compressed air systems efficiently. It employs desiccants—materials with high adsorption capacity—to extract water vapor from the air. In this system, one part of the dryer is in the drying phase while the other is regenerating through a portion of the dried air, which is released without the need for external energy sources, resulting in minimal operational costs.</p><p>The Heatless Regenerative Desiccant Dryer Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.5% during the forecast period.
