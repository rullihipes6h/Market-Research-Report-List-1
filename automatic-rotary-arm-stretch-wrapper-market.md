<p><p>The Automatic Rotary Arm Stretch Wrapper market comprises two main types: Semi-Automatic and Fully-Automatic systems. In contrast, Fully-Automatic wrappers operate independently, handling the entire wrapping process without manual intervention, ideal for high-volume operations. Both types enhance packaging efficiency, reduce labor costs, and ensure consistent load stability, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
