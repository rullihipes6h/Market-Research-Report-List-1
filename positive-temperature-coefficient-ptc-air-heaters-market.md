<p><p>Positive Temperature Coefficient (PTC) air heaters are innovative heating devices that utilize PTC thermistors to provide efficient and self-regulating heat. These heaters operate on the principle that as the temperature increases, their resistance also increases, leading to a decrease in current draw. This unique feature allows them to maintain a steady temperature without the risk of overheating, making them ideal for various applications, including residential, commercial, and industrial settings.</p><p>The PTC air heaters market is witnessing significant growth, driven by increased demand for energy-efficient heating solutions and advancements in technology.
