<p><strong>The De-Ionization Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dual-Beds</li><li>Mixed-Beds</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The de-ionization service market can be categorized into dual-bed and mixed-bed systems. Dual-bed systems utilize two separate tanks, one for cation exchange resin and the other for anion exchange resin, ensuring effective ion removal but requiring regeneration of each bed individually. In contrast, mixed-bed systems combine both resins in a single tank, providing higher purity water due to the synergistic effect and reducing space requirements. Both types serve industries needing high-quality deionized water for various applications.</p></p>
