<p><p>The dual sport tires market is categorized primarily into tubeless tires and solid tires. They also minimize the risk of flats, providing increased reliability. Solid tires, on the other hand, are designed for enhanced durability and puncture resistance, making them suitable for rugged terrains. Together, these tire types cater to the diverse needs of dual sport enthusiasts seeking versatility and performance.</p></p>
