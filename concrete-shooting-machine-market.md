<p><ul><li>Wet Shooting</li><li>Dry Shooting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The concrete shooting machine market is primarily divided into two types: wet shooting and dry shooting. Wet shooting involves the use of a moisture-rich mix, enhancing adhesion and reducing dust, making it ideal for applications requiring strong bonding, such as tunnels and slopes. In contrast, dry shooting uses a dry mixture of concrete, relying on high-velocity air for application. This method is efficient for projects needing precise control without excess moisture, often suited for repairs and reinforcement work.</p></p>
