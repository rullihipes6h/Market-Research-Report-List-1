<p><strong>The Ground Source Heat Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Exchange (DX)</li><li>Closed Loop</li><li>Open Loop</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ground Source Heat Pump market includes three main types: Direct Exchange (DX), Closed Loop, and Open Loop systems. </p><p>DX systems use refrigerant-filled copper coils directly buried underground to transfer heat, leading to efficient energy exchange. Closed Loop systems circulate a fluid through a network of underground pipes, offering stable temperatures for heating and cooling. Open Loop systems utilize groundwater directly, pumping it through the system and returning it afterward. Each type serves distinct applications, with varying installation and operational costs.</p></p>
