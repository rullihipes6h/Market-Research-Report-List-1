<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Immunochemistry Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chemiluminescence Immunoassay (CLIA) Analyzers</li><li>Immuno Fluorescence (IFA) Analyzers</li><li>Enzyme Immunoassay (EIA) Analyzers</li><li>Radioimmunoassay (RIA) Analyzers</li><li>Enzyme Linked Fluorescent Assay (ELFA) Systems</li><li>Multiplexed Assay Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The immunochemistry analyzer market encompasses various types of analyzers based on different methodologies. Chemiluminescence Immunoassay (CLIA) analyzers use luminescent reactions for detection, while Immunofluorescence (IFA) analyzers exploit fluorescent tags for visualizing antigens. Enzyme Immunoassay (EIA) analyzers utilize enzyme-substrate reactions for quantifying substances. Radioimmunoassay (RIA) analyzers employ radioisotopes to measure antigen-antibody interactions. Enzyme Linked Fluorescent Assay (ELFA) systems combine EIA principles with fluorescent detection, and Multiplexed Assay Systems enable simultaneous analysis of multiple targets for enhanced efficiency.</p></p>
