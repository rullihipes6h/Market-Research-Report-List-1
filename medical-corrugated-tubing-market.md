<p><p>The medical corrugated tubing market comprises two main types: plastic corrugated tubing and metal corrugated tubing. Plastic corrugated tubing is lightweight, flexible, and resistant to chemical reactions, making it ideal for various medical applications, including drainage and fluid transfer. In contrast, metal corrugated tubing offers superior durability and is often used in applications requiring high pressure and temperature resistance. Both types are essential for ensuring safe and efficient medical procedures while providing structural integrity and adaptability.</p></p>
