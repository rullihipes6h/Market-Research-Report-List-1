<p><p>The Assam black tea market is primarily divided into two main types: Orthodox tea and CTC (Crush, Tear, Curl) tea. Orthodox tea is produced using traditional methods, resulting in whole leaf tea known for its nuanced flavors and aromas, often preferred by tea connoisseurs. Conversely, CTC tea is produced through a machine process that cuts leaves into small pieces, leading to a brisk and robust flavor, favored for its strength and quicker brewing time, often used in mass-market tea bags.</p></p>
