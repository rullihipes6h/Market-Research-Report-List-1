<p><p>The optoelectronic probe card market is divided into two main types: MEMS (Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems) and Non-MEMS. MEMS probe cards utilize microfabrication techniques to create miniature mechanical structures, offering high precision and flexibility in testing. They are especially suited for advanced packaging and high-frequency applications. Non-MEMS probe cards, on the other hand, involve traditional designs that may offer robustness and simplicity, suitable for a broader range of semiconductor testing needs. Both types serve critical roles in the semiconductor manufacturing process.</p></p>
