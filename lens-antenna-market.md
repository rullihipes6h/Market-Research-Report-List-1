<p><p>The lens antenna market features primarily two types: Horn Lens Antennas and Luneberg Lens Antennas. Horn Lens Antennas utilize a horn-shaped design to direct electromagnetic waves, enhancing gain and radiation pattern control, making them suitable for radar and satellite communications. Luneberg Lens Antennas, characterized by their spherical design, achieve low-profile structures and high efficiency, enabling wideband applications. Both types serve diverse sectors, including telecommunications, aerospace, and defense, driven by the demand for advanced communication technologies and high-performance systems.</p></p>
