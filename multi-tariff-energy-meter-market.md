<p><p>The Multi-Tariff Energy Meter market is divided into Single Phase and Three Phase types. Single Phase meters are commonly used in residential applications, measuring energy consumption from a single alternating current (AC) phase, ideal for low power demands. In contrast, Three Phase meters are utilized in commercial and industrial settings, measuring energy across three phases, enabling more efficient power distribution and management. Both types facilitate flexible billing based on variable tariffs, promoting energy conservation and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
