<p><strong>The Photopheresis Products Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open System</li><li>Closed System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photopheresis products market is divided into two main types: open systems and closed systems. Open systems allow for the collection and processing of blood outside the body, exposing it to ultraviolet light before returning it to the patient. This method enables flexibility but may pose contamination risks. In contrast, closed systems involve a sealed environment for blood handling, minimizing contamination and improving safety. Each system has its applications and benefits, catering to different clinical needs in photopheresis treatments.</p></p>
