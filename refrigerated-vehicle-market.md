<p><p>The refrigerated vehicle market is primarily divided into two segments: frozen and chilled markets. Frozen vehicles maintain temperatures below freezing to transport items like ice cream, frozen meats, and seafood, ensuring long-term preservation. In contrast, chilled vehicles operate at temperatures slightly above freezing, ideal for perishable goods such as dairy products, fresh fruits, and vegetables, which require a stable, cooler environment to remain fresh. Both segments cater to the growing demand for safe and efficient delivery of temperature-sensitive products.</p></p>
