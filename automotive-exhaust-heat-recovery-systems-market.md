<p><p>Automotive exhaust heat recovery systems improve vehicle efficiency by utilizing waste heat from the exhaust. Exhaust Gas Heat Recovery (EGHR) captures and repurposes waste heat for various purposes. Rankine Cycle Systems convert heat into mechanical energy using a working fluid, enhancing overall efficiency. Thermoelectric Generators harness temperature differences to generate electricity. Electric Turbo Compounding (ETC) combines turbocharging with electric power to recover exhaust energy, boosting performance and efficiency. Together, these technologies optimize fuel consumption and reduce emissions in vehicles.</p></p>
