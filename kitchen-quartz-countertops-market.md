<p><strong>The Kitchen Quartz Countertops Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Casting Molding</li><li>Press Molding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The kitchen quartz countertops market is primarily categorized into two types: casting molding and press molding. Casting molding involves mixing quartz crystals with resin and pouring the mixture into molds, allowing for intricate designs and seamless surfaces. This method offers flexibility in shapes and colors. In contrast, press molding utilizes high pressure to form quartz slabs, creating a denser material with a more uniform appearance. Both techniques enable the production of durable, aesthetically pleasing countertops suitable for various kitchen styles.</p></p>
